Who Is ADJ?

Hi! My name is Andre Dudley Jr.—the ADJ in question. I am the creator of the Dev.ADJ website. When I graduated from Albany State University with a Computer Science degree, I had to ask myself the age-old question:

Now what?

A question, to be brutally honest, I was in no shape to even think about answering. The past two years, before I had even begun to think about making this website, had been brutal. And, as a result, I had little projects to show for my four years of education. I could code—at least I though I could—but I never really knew what to do with what I learned.

There were a few concepts and ideas I had in mind, but nothing really stuck. And, in the wake of my own mental health crisis that I had gone through, an idea bloomed. Having remembered the occasional heartache—and headaches—that I had endured through my own current journey of learning web development, I came up with the idea of cataloging my own learning journey.

I decided to log my own learning and growth as a way to show people (and my future self) that if I can do it, you DEFINITELY can. So, if I am able to help even ONE person in a concept that I used to struggle in, I'd consider the existence of this website to be a massive success. Or, if one of my yap sessions about my hobbies ends up making your day, my day will be made too.

I love each and every one of you,

ADJ
